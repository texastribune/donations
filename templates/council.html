We’re jump-starting a new model to fund independent investigative journalism.

The Texas Tribune pioneered a new model for journalism— nonprofit and nonpartisan. Now we’re pioneering a new model to fund this specific type of high-impact investigative reporting. That’s why we’re reaching out to you — a business leader who knows how work gets done — to be one of our founders.

We’re creating a new model where business leaders can support action that makes Texas better through investigative reporting.

A new kind of funding — a business leader coalition

The Council is an organization of business leaders committed to long-term support of the investigative reporting process. Why business leaders? Because you are builders who are comfortable with a different kind of action and a different timeline:

  • Investing big today to achieve upsized returns over time.
  • Know that results need time and space to mature and drive real results.

Long-term focus

The Council is a new form of patient, long-view funding that lets us do the work that matters.

  • Stable funding to support stellar teams of the best journalists and build their careers
  • Protection from short-term budgets, politics, annual fundraisers, and trendy media ad models
  • Fully independent

We’re asking for multi-year commitments (in exchange for a multi-year Council seat). As a business leader, you know the operational efficiencies and quality we can create with predictable funding.

100% dedicated to investigative reporting

We’re insulating the reporters from the daily pressures of the newsroom and funding to focus on long-term programs that pay off richly:

  • Support the journalists doing cutting-edge investigative work.
  • Enable long-timeline deep investigations.
  • Cover Texas state-wide to produce stories with maximum impact.

Lead a pioneering program to fund independent investigative journalism that drives accountability and ends corruption. Join the Texas Tribune Investigative Reporting Council to make your impact.

Meaningful participation for members

In exchange for this form of investment, we’re creating an opportunity for you to be more than just a donor. You’ll be a member of the Texas Tribune Investigative Reporting Council:

  • A great credential that shows your leadership
  • An inside view into the fascinating investigative process
  • A carefully chosen network of leaders like you
