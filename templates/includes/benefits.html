Benefits by giving level

Member - Donate up to $49/year

  • Our official Texas Tribune member decal.
  • Behind-the-scenes access to the Texas Tribune newsroom, including Q&As with our reporters and sneak peeks at special projects via our monthly member newsletter and quarterly stakeholder reports.
  • Our utmost gratitude and a special place on our growing donor wall.

Informed member - Donate $50 to $99/year

Benefits include all of the above, plus special perks at Texas Tribune Festival, our annual meeting of the brightest minds in politics and policy.

Engaged member - Donate $100 to $499/year

Benefits include all of the above, plus invites to fun exclusive experiences geared toward connecting you with political insiders, Tribune community members and industry colleagues.

Involved member - Donate $500 to $999/year

Benefits include all of the above, plus discounted rental rates for our Studio 919 event space.

Giving circles

  • Leadership Circle - $2,500/year for three years
  • Chairman’s Circle - $5,000/year for three years
