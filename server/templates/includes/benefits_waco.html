Be a founding donor this year.

Individuals, businesses, organizations and institutions that donate for the Waco Local News Initiative via The Texas Tribune in 2024 are Founding Donors of the Waco Local News Initiative.

These donors contribute financially to the initiative during its initial launch phase.

The Waco Local News Initiative plans to recognize all founding donors in an online donor wall upon launch. Because all donations are listed publicly for transparency purposes, anonymous gifts are discouraged.

The Waco Local News Initiative will develop membership, donation and sponsorship policies consistent with the standards of philanthropic and journalistic integrity.

Founding benefactors

  • A pledge of $10,000 per year or more for 3 years

Founders circle

  • A pledge from $1,000 to $9,999 per year for 3 years

Founding neighbor members

  • A pledge from $12 to $999 per year for 3 years

For more information about becoming a founding donor to the Waco Local News Initiative, contact the Texas Tribune Senior Major Gifts Officer James Canup at james.canup@texastribune.org or (512) 658-4516.
