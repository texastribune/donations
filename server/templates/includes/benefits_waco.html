Be a founding donor this year.

Individuals, businesses, organizations and institutions donating to the Waco Local News Initiative via The Texas Tribune in 2024 will be recognized as founding donors, who will be recognized upon our launch.

The Waco Local News Initiative will develop membership, donation and sponsorship policies consistent with the standards of philanthropic and journalistic integrity.

Founding benefactors

  • A commitment of $10,000 or more per year

Founders circle

  • A commitment from $1,000 to $9,999 per year

Founding neighbor members

  • A commitment from $12 to $999 per year

For more information about becoming a founding donor to the Waco Local News Initiative, contact James Canup, The Texas Tribune's senior major gifts officer, at james.canup@texastribune.org or (512) 658-4516.
